About

Who I am and how I can help

I combine a strong engineering background with a pragmatic view on business needs. My goal is to make releases predictable and safe, not slow.

For more than 10 years I worked with industrial automation, sensors and complex technical projects. I led tenders, integrated solutions from different vendors and communicated with engineers, managers and end-customers.

This experience taught me to ask the right questions, see risks early and translate business language into technical tasks. Now I apply the same mindset to software quality and test automation.

I’m especially interested in projects where quality really matters: B2B products, complex backends, data-driven systems.
